About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate at the National University of Singapore (NUS), specializing in the intersection of high-precision hardware and real-time industrial data.

Currently, I engineer advanced motion and vision control platforms for laser micro-processing at JD Union. I focus on integrating hardware and software—like galvanometer scanners and motion stages—to optimize industrial 3D printing.

Previously, I built end-to-end telemetry pipelines for A*STAR (ARTC) to monitor metal 3D printers using InfluxDB and Grafana. Whether I am designing joint mechanisms for exoskeletons or routing custom IIoT sensor instrumentation, my goal is to build systems that are mechanically robust and highly data-driven.
